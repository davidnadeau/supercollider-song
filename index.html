Here I will describe the structure, techniques, and effects in my composition. As well as detail about the instruments I've created.

Structure of Song

The song is 2 minutes in length, composed of sounds and instruments.

It begins with 10 seconds of a custom synth called the SinPulser. The SinPulser takes no parameters, and oscilates the freqency of a sine wave by sending the frequency through 2 rate based value generators. The order of value generators and the final processing through a reverb generator leads to a very eiry mysterious sound.

It then plays a chiptune like song for around 18 seconds using a custom chiptune synth. The frequency of the notes are based on a supercollider scale in attempts create a musical sounding pattern. The chiptune synth takes 2 parameters. The frequency of the note, and the frequency of the amplitude pulse. The larger the amplitude pulse, the more the notes will overlap. Using a pulse wave, a pulse oscilator is used to create grains of the pulse wave. The pulse grain has reverb applied and is enveloped.

Then 20 seconds of ambient noises created with another custom synth. This synth is called ambientBackground and has 3 parts. The first part is a saw wave with the frequency chosen at a rate by a random value generator. Reverb is added to this sound. This was used to create strange quite complex sounds. Then a sine wave is used to create more simple constant oscilating noise. The frequency of the sine wave is chosen at a rate by a random value generator at a frequency determined by pink noise. Lastly a pulse wave is used, with frequencies genrated at a rate by a random value generator at a frequency determnined by pink noise. All these noises then have reverb added to them. The result is ominous and dynamic, rarely ever sounding the exact same.
